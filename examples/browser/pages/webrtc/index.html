WebRTC DataChannel (manual SDP)

Two browser instances on a LAN, peer-to-peer chat, no kernel.

How to use

  1. Both peers load this page on their respective machines.
  2. A clicks Create offer. After a few seconds, A's local SDP appears.
  3. A copies their local SDP and sends it to B over any out-of-band channel (chat, email, Signal — it's plain text).
  4. B pastes A's SDP into Remote SDP and clicks Accept offer. After a few seconds, B's local SDP (the answer) appears.
  5. B sends their SDP back to A.
  6. A pastes B's SDP into Remote SDP and clicks Accept answer.
  7. The DataChannel opens on both sides. Type messages to chat peer-to-peer.
